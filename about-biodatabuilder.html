What makes us
special —

Our Writers - What ultimately makes us stand out are the talented writers we've assembled as our writing team. Each writer that joins our team is not only experienced and well-qualified, but has also passed our vigorous screening process that tests their actual ability to write top of the line resumes.

Our Process - Most resume services will highlight the fact that you can work one-on-one with your writer - but most of them avoid mentioning the fact that they rarely allow clients to have actual in-depth connversations with their writers over the phone.

Our individualized process ensures that you'll get the time to address all the concerns and questions you have with your resume before your writer works on your resume and after the resume is written. On top of that, our process ensures that you'll end up with a satisfactory resume by offering unlimited revisions - a feature resume services only offer if you are able provide proof that you've applied to jobs and failed to receive interviews.

Our Care - Your success is our success. While other resume services tend to overload their writers with too many clients to handle, we ensure that our writers are given at most, 3 clients to handle at any point in time. This ensures that they are given ample time to work with each individual client and perfect each resume they write.
